③ Deep-metric quick reference (what these "xG-like" metrics each represent)

xG / npxG: the total quality of shooting chances; excluding penalties, it better reflects open-play creativity.
xGA / xGC: the quality of chances opponents create against you, measuring true defensive level.
xGD / 90: xG−xGA, the single best proxy for overall strength.
xG per shot: the quality of each shot — a low value = lots of long-range shots / poor chances.
PPDA: passes allowed per defensive action; lower = more aggressive pressing.
Field tilt: the share of final-third touches, measuring territorial dominance rather than pure possession.
