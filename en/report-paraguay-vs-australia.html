📋 Quick Summary (Read This First)

This is a Group D final round, a direct do-or-die with "asymmetric qualification motivation". The USA have sealed top spot on 6 points and Türkiye are out on 0; Paraguay and Australia are level on 3 points contesting the second direct qualification ticket. The two teams have not met across the first two rounds, but on goal difference Australia are 0 and Paraguay -2 — so if the two draw and both reach 4 points, the first tiebreaker (head-to-head) is null because this match is the head-to-head and a draw makes no difference, so it goes straight to goal difference, where Australia have the edge. Hence a draw favours Australia and secures their 2nd-place qualification; Paraguay basically must win (a win means 2nd-place qualification; a draw or narrow loss leaves them hoping for one of the 8 "best third-placed" spots, dependent on other groups' results, out of their hands). ③ Deep-metric quick reference (what these "xG-like" metrics each represent)

xG / npxG: the total quality of shooting chances; stripping out penalties better reflects open-play creativity.
xGA / xGC: the quality of chances the opponent creates against you; measures true defensive level.
xGD / 90: xG−xGA, the single best proxy for overall quality.
xG per shot: the quality of each shot — a low value = many long-range efforts / poor chances.
PPDA: passes allowed per defensive action; the lower the value the more aggressive the press.
Field tilt: share of final-third touches; measures territorial tilt rather than raw possession.
